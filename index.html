About UpEdu

Founded in 2015, UpEdu Education Center is dedicated to providing high-quality after-school education services to families in the San Francisco Bay Area. Our educational philosophy is "holistic development and personalized training."

We have an experienced team of teachers. All teachers have relevant subject backgrounds and teaching qualifications, and their average teaching experience is over 8 years.

Our classrooms are safe and comfortable, equipped with advanced teaching equipment and rich learning resources to create the best learning experience for students.
